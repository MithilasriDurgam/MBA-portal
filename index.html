Job Description

We are seeking a highly motivated and experienced Senior Human Resources Executive to lead and implement strategic HR initiatives that align with our organization’s goals and culture. This role requires a proactive and people-centric leader who can drive talent acquisition, employee engagement, performance management, and HR policy development to build a strong and dynamic workforce. As a key member of the HR leadership team, the ideal candidate will play a crucial role in shaping the company’s human capital strategy by fostering a positive and inclusive work environment, ensuring compliance with employment regulations, and driving continuous improvement. This position demands exceptional leadership, problem-solving, and decision-making skills, along with the ability to adapt to a fast-paced and evolving business landscape.

Key Responsibilities

  • Talent Acquisition & Onboarding: Oversee recruitment, hiring, and onboarding to attract top talent.
  • HR Policies & Compliance: Ensure adherence to labor laws, company policies, and best HR practices.
  • Training & Development: Organize employee learning programs to enhance skills and productivity.
  • Compensation & Benefits: Manage payroll, incentives, and employee welfare programs.
  • Employee Relations: Address employee concerns, mediate conflicts, and maintain a positive work culture.
  • HR Data & Analytics: Use HR metrics to improve workforce planning and decision-making.

Preferred Skills and Requirements

  • Bachelor’s/Master’s degree in Human Resources, Business Administration, or related field.
  • Minimum of 5 years of progressive HR experience, with at least 2 years in a leadership role.
  • Strong knowledge of employment laws, HR best practices, and talent management strategies.
  • Excellent communication, negotiation, and interpersonal skills.
  • Proficiency in HRIS and other HR-related software.
  • Ability to handle confidential information with discretion and professionalism.
